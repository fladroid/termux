Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Tracker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

No Data Collection

Tracker does not collect any personal information. The app has no internet permission and cannot communicate with any server, analytics service, or third party.

Data Storage

All data you enter — activity counts, notes, and settings — is stored locally on your device in a SQLite database. This data is never transmitted anywhere.

You can export your data at any time as a CSV or JSON file, and delete it using the Reset function in Settings.

No Ads, No Tracking

Tracker contains no advertising, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no third-party SDKs of any kind. There is nothing in this app that observes your behavior.

No Account Required

Tracker requires no registration, no login, and no email address. You can use the app completely anonymously.

Permissions

Tracker requests only the permissions necessary for its core features:

• Storage — to allow export and import of your data to local files.

The app does not request internet, location, contacts, camera, or any other sensitive permissions.

Children's Privacy

Tracker does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new date.
